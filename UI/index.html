About Us

Found in 1997, Cadrex Overseas Ltd. is a leading industrial paper supplier serving the Middle East, Gulf States, Southeast Asia, Northern and Eastern Africa, South Korea, and parts of Europe. Post-Covid-19, we recognized the vital importance of staple foods and food products to human life, viewing this sector as more than just business - it’s a mission to nourish and enrich communities worldwide. Thus, Alaya Ltd., Co., a Cadrex subsidiary, was founded to serve the food industry, fostering food security and prosperity through connecting qualify global suppliers and customers.
